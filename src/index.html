Ways to train

Choose your challenge

Whether you want to burn fat, build muscle, improve endurance, get expert guidance or simply do your own thing, we have options for you.

  • Gym Floor

    Use our full facility independently. The gym floor is packed with functional training options, a full set of free weights and cable machines, Olympic lifting platforms, bodyweight equipment and cardio equipment.

  • HIIT Programs

    High-intensity interval training programs that burn calories during and after your workout. Perfect for those optimizing their time with 30-45 minute sessions. Separate training area with HIIT-specific tools.

  • Personal Training

    A great way to stay committed to your goals with one-on-one coaching tailored to you. Whether you intend to get in shape, improve athletic performance or recover from injury, our qualified and experienced PTs can help you get there.

  • Group Classes

    There's nothing like keeping up with a group to get pumped! Explore new movements, have fun while working up a sweat or make new friends in our boutique-style fitness classes that offer something for everyone.
