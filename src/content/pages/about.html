Our Story

The gym where everyone knows your name.

Fitness3K opened in Melbourne in 2018 because we were tired of impersonal big-box gyms where you're just another membership number. We wanted to create a space that feels more like a local pub than a corporate fitness chain.

Founded by Melbourne local James Chen, a former Olympic weightlifting coach, Fitness3K was built on a simple idea: quality equipment and expert guidance shouldn't be exclusive to elite athletes. Whether you're walking into a gym for the first time or training for competition, you'll get the same attention, the same gear, and the same supportive community.

Seven years on, we're proud to be Melbourne's friendliest gym—the kind of place where trainers remember not just your name, but how your week's been. No judgement. No pressure. Just real people helping friends get stronger.

"I wanted to build a gym that feels like your local—where regulars look out for each other and newcomers are welcomed. Whether you train at 4am or 11pm, you're part of the Fitness3K family." James Chen Founder
Meet the Team

What makes us different

No lock-in contracts: Month-to-month memberships only. We earn your loyalty through results, not fine print.

24/7 access: Train when it suits your schedule.

Equipment-first approach: We invest in the best gear available, not fancy extras that don't serve you.

Real coaches: All trainers hold university qualifications in Exercise Science or related fields as a minimum.

Community focused: Regular social events, challenges, and a supportive atmosphere where everyone feels welcome.

  • Strength Equipment

    Rogue Monster rack systems, Eleiko Olympic barbells, complete dumbbell range to 60kg, adjustable benches, cable machines with multiple attachment points.

  • Cardio Machines

    Concept2 Model D rowers, Assault AirBikes, Woodway 4Front treadmills with incline capability, Schwinn Airdyne bikes for interval training.

  • Functional Training

    TRX suspension trainers, kettlebells from 8kg to 48kg, battle ropes, plyo boxes in multiple heights, medicine balls, resistance bands.

  • Recovery

    Theragun PRO massage devices, high-density foam rollers, stretching area with yoga mats, mobility tools, trigger point equipment for self-massage.
