Group Exercise Classes

Train with us – 24 classes every week

Try a new movement, push your limits and discover new strengths in our group classes. These high-energy sessions are led by qualified coaches in a studio that can accommodate 25 people. All fitness levels are welcome and modified movements are provided for beginners.

  • HIIT Strength

    45 min
    High-intensity circuit combining resistance training with cardio bursts. Build muscle while torching calories. Uses barbells, dumbbells and bodyweight exercises. 45 minutes of maximum effort with built-in rest periods. Burns 400–600 calories.

  • Functional Fitness

    60 min
    Real-world movement patterns that improve how you move in daily life. Think lifting, carrying, pushing, pulling and moving in multiple directions. Perfect for building practical strength, mobility and preventing injuries. Uses kettlebells, battle ropes, medicine balls and bodyweight exercises.

  • Cardio Blast

    50 min
    Pure cardiovascular conditioning using rowers, exercise bikes and bodyweight cardio drills. Interval-based training that improves heart health and endurance while burning serious calories. Mix of steady-state cardio and high-intensity intervals. Modifications available so you work at your own intensity.

  • Mobility & Recovery

    60 min
    Active recovery sessions focusing on flexibility, joint health and injury prevention. Combines yoga-style stretches, foam rolling techniques, breathwork and corrective exercises. The perfect complement to intense training days—helps you recover faster and move better.
